What is Passwordless Authentication?

Passwordless authentication is a method of verifying a user's identity without requiring them to provide a traditional password. Instead of relying on passwords—which are often vulnerable to attacks like phishing, brute force, or leaks—it uses more secure alternatives such as:

- Biometric authentication (e.g., fingerprints, face recognition)

- One-Time Codes sent via email, SMS, or authentication apps

- Hardware security keys (like YubiKey)

- Passkeys and cryptographic solutions that leverage public-key infrastructure (PKI)
